About Little

Our mission at The Little App is simple. We want to raise happier kids. Based on your child's age, Little can offer you age-appropriate activities, videos & story ideas, so you never run out of fun things to do with your child again.

Little was founded by Tony Hsieh & Cathy Hsu in the spring of 2013, inspired by their daughter Wynter. We're grateful to all the parents & community members for helping us turn this app idea into reality.
